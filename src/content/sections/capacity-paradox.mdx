<div class="capacity-content-box">
  <p class="capacity-intro">An SDR can handle 3-5 new prospects daily while maintaining their pipeline—each requiring 15-20 activities to convert.</p>

  <p>With 60-100 activities daily across their entire pipeline, that's the realistic limit. To hit growth targets, you need 50+ new prospects worked daily.</p>

  <p class="capacity-conclusion">The math doesn't work with one person. Hiring more SDRs scales linearly—each rep costs $60-80K plus recruitment, training, and turnover risk.</p>
